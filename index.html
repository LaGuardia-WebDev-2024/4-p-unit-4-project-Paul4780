Why I Want To Go To Switzerland

Hiking In The Mountains

I've never gone on any hikes because where I live theres nowhere that has good views at the top. However Switzerland is made for hikes and great scenery.

The World Famous Chocolate and Cheese

As someone who has a sweet tooth and hearing about a country who is famous for the best type of sweet it makes me want to go there that much more.

Sking In The Alps

Ive only went skiing once with my family when I was in elementary school and I dont remember it well but I remeber the joy of going faster than I ever have before on my own two feet. With how much older I am now theres no way that I wouldnt have even more fun than when I was a little kid.
